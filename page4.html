About

Website Purpose

Why did I create this site?

The obvious answer is that I did it for a grade. Other than this, though, I wanted to create a website that allows myself and others to consider my approaches to media making as an individual, since I pride much of my work from my identity and experiences, to provide perspectives and visibility to subjects I find imporant. This is not to say that all of my work has a deeper message or anything, I do take advantage of the liberty to create as a way to push my boundaries and explore new approached to the art of digital media.

Where will I go from here?

Thanks to this class I have properly learned to create my own website from scratch. Even though I am not fully satisfied with the layouts and overall design of my site, I will not deny the artistic capabilities I've yet to explore in coding HTML and CSS. I plan to come back to better my stylistic practices for putting together an appealing website.
